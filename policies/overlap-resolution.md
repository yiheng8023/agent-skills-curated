# Overlap Resolution Policy

Semantic intersection is expected; unresolved ownership is not. Every obvious
overlap group must record one default owner, scoped alternatives, conditions,
fallbacks, conflicts, merged sources, and excluded candidates.

Source-selection schema 1 uses `adopt`; third-party admission uses `approve`.
Both use `merge`, `adapter-only`, `recipe-only`, or `reject` for non-standalone
outcomes. Never install two globally triggered routers or a third general TDD,
debugging, review, planning, frontend, Git, or security workflow merely because
its wording differs.

Overlap review governs third-party Skill-body admission and uses an abstract,
product-neutral capability taxonomy. Official, runtime-owned, built-in, and
first-party Skill bodies may be examined only as evidence captured with a
review date. They are not governed or managed inventory in this repository,
and the evidence must not be treated as current runtime availability or
canonical ownership.
