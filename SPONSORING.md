# Sponsoring

Sponsorship is voluntary. Any amount is appreciated and supports ongoing maintenance, source review, documentation, testing, and community operations.

Reviewed sponsorship channels shared by the repository owner:

- CNY: use the WeChat Pay or Alipay QR codes shown in [README.md](README.md).
- Cross-border or other supported currencies: use [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/ncp/payment/LNTF8KXGJXMZY). Available currencies, payment methods, conversion, and fees are determined by the checkout page and may vary by country or region.

Verify the displayed recipient before confirming a payment. Do not send funds through accounts, links, or QR codes that are not published by the repository owner.

Sponsorship is not a charitable donation or tax deduction and does not purchase a support SLA, security warranty, review priority, admission, release timing, feature commitment, exception from governance, or influence over technical decisions.
